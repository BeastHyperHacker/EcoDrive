Reduce carbon emmssions and save money.

Car emissions cause harmful carbon dioxide that causes the earth to warm up.
These emissions come from the petrol cars that we drive.
These cars also come with the price of having to pay for gas.
To solve these problems, you can use an electric car that emits no carbon.
You also pay much less for power than you pay for gas.
As the cost of electric vehicles reduce and become more accessible, the environment and your wallet will feel much better.
